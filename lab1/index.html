Protecting our enviornment

Nature is in crisis. Climate change is driving record-breaking heatwaves, raging wildfires and devastating floods, leaving destruction in its wake. Meanwhile, forests are torn down, oceans are polluted and precious ecosystems are pushed to the brink, as faceless corporations rip through biodiverse habitats and communities for profit.

  • Keep yourself informed the more you know the better you can help
  • be political, vote for someone who is a representative of your views and makes the enviornment a top priority
  • Travel responsibly, Walk or cycle when you can and take public transport for longer commutes
